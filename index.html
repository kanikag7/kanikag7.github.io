Kanika Goswami

I am a Research Assistant at Adobe Research, San Jose. My research focuses on LLM for Chart Understanding, developing multimodal LLM multi-agent frameworks to automate chart analysis verification, visualization generation, and chart editing. I am also collaborating with the University of Maryland College Park on Multimodal RAG frameworks.

Previously, I worked as a Software Development Engineer at Microsoft and Adobe. I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW) with distinction (Top 5% in class).
